Work in Progress

A very short history about me, I was born in Stockholm Sweden, where I went to school and was always interested in art. I later moved to the US and ended up going to school at School of Visual Arts (SVA) in New York City. I now live in Fort Lauderdale Florida, plus spending more time in Stockholm, where I trying my best to balance my fine art sculpting with my profession in graphic design.

During Covid-19, I started to reproduce some old sculptures in hydrocal (artist plaster) and paint them with acrylic, also creating new sculptures in clay. Why so late in life you might ask, the simple answer, passion, and the feeling of time standing still while creating my art.

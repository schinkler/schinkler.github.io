About Us
& Our Team
Greg Barnes

CEO and Founder of Physician Services. Greg began his work in chiropractic as a coach and consultant. After traveling the country for 5 years, Greg recognized that billing was one of the biggest challenges facing most practices. Understanding this, Greg launched Physician Services in 1990. 30 years later, Physician Services provides comprehensive billing services to hundreds of chiropractic and multidisciplinary practices across the country.





Our Team

We consists of over a dozen professional chiropractic billers and their assistants. With over 100 years of combined experience, our diverse team of billers have experience with all major chiropractic software systems and insurance carriers in all 50 states.

You’ll work with a dedicated billing specialist that will help you maximize your insurance collections and help you increase revenue and profitability within your practice! Unlike many billing solutions, we’re not a one or two-man show. We’ll assign you the best billing specialist that aligns best with your practice needs. Whether you’re switching from in-house billing or changing billing providers, you’ll never need to worry about your biller taking a vacation, moving on, or failing to perform. Billing is our expertise, and your success is our success! Allow one of our experts to become an integrated part of your team and experience the Physician Services difference!
